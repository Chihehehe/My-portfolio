MYOB's DevelopHer program represents exactly what the tech industry needs‚Äîa pathway specifically designed to bring more women into software development. What makes this program stand out is its recognition that talent exists in diverse places and doesn't always follow traditional paths.

Why I Want to Join DevelopHer

DevelopHer offers exactly what I'm looking for: a structured pathway into software engineering that values my potential over my past experience. I'm drawn to the program's commitment to not just teaching technical skills, but fostering an environment where women can thrive in tech.

As someone deeply passionate about coding and building solutions, I'm excited about the opportunity to learn from industry professionals while working on real projects. The program's emphasis on practical experience aligns perfectly with my hands-on learning style.

Most importantly, I want to be part of the change in the tech industry. By joining DevelopHer, I'll not only advance my own career but also contribute to creating a more diverse and inclusive tech landscape for the future.
